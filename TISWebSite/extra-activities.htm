مجلة المدرسة

Benefits

Your child gets to explore his/her physical, creative, social, political, and career interests with like-minded people. They will find friends: Trying something different may bring your child in contact with other children you didn't know who share the same interests and curiosity.

So many choices can seem overwhelming, but getting involved in new activities with new people is a fun way to challenge your child.
